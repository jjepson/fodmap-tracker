# Low FODMAP Meal Tracker

A simple, mobile-friendly app to track FODMAP load throughout the day and log gut symptoms.

## What is FODMAP?

FODMAP stands for **Fermentable Oligosaccharides, Disaccharides, Monosaccharides, And Polyols** — a group of carbohydrates that can trigger IBS symptoms in sensitive people. A low-FODMAP diet is an evidence-based approach to managing IBS and digestive issues.

This tracker helps you:
- **Log meals** throughout the day with instant FODMAP scoring
- **Track symptoms** and see patterns over time
- **Adjust serving sizes** on the fly with support for cups, ounces, and grams
- **Edit entries** without deleting and re-adding
- **Save favorite meals** for quick re-use
- **View history** to understand what you ate and how you felt

## Features

- **300+ foods** from Monash University and FODMAP Friendly research
- **Raw & cooked protein options** with automatic weight conversion
- **Herbs & spices category** with safe/unsafe designations
- **Flexible serving sizes** — type any amount in native units, grams, or ounces
- **Inline editing** — tap the pencil icon to adjust an entry
- **Symptom logging** on a 5-point scale (Rough → Great)
- **Saved meals** — star any meal to add it back in one tap
- **Monash fallback** — can't find a food? Direct link to the Monash app
- **No account needed** — data stays on your phone using browser storage

## Getting Started

1. Open the link in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android)
2. Tap **+ Add** to start logging a meal
3. Search for a food or scroll by category
4. Tap **Add** and choose your serving size
5. Tap the green/yellow/red score to see the FODMAP level
6. Rate your symptoms in the **Log** tab at end of day
7. Review your history in the **History** tab

### Adding Custom Foods

Don't see a food? Tap **+ Custom** to add your own:
- Name and serving size (e.g., "1 bowl" or "150g")
- Estimated calories
- FODMAP level (Low/Med/High)

Your custom foods save for next time.

## Editing & Deleting

- **Edit a serving**: Tap the ✏️ pencil icon next to the food
- **Delete**: Tap the ✕ button or use the delete option in edit mode
- **Save a meal**: Tap ⭐ on any meal card to save it for quick re-use

## Data & Privacy

- **All data stays on your phone** — nothing is sent to a server
- Data is stored in browser storage and persists between visits
- If you clear your browser data, your history will be deleted
- Each device/browser is separate (phone won't sync with laptop)

## If a Food Isn't Listed

The app includes ~300 verified foods from Monash University testing. For foods not in the list, the **Monash FODMAP app** ($10 one-time purchase) has ~900 tested items and is the most authoritative source.

Tap **Check Monash app** in the search results for a direct link.

## Tips

- **Before cooking**: Log protein by raw weight — the app does the cook-down math
- **Batch entry**: Save meals you eat regularly (e.g., "Tuesday breakfast") so you can add them all at once
- **Track symptoms**: Log how you felt each day — over time you'll see which foods trigger your symptoms
- **Check ingredients**: Condiments and sauces often have hidden garlic/onion — read the label
